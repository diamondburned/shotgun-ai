when we can kill:
- opp has zero blocks, we have knife out or bullets loaded
- opp reload with knife out, we have knife out or 2+ bullets loaded 
- opp reload without knife out, we have knife out or 1+ bullets loaded
- opp knife out, we have knife out or 1+ bullets loaded


when we can die:
- we have zero blocks, opp has knife out or bullets loaded
- we reload with knife out, opp has knife out or 2+ bullets loaded 
- we reload without knife out, opp has knife out or 1+ bullets loaded
- we knife out, opp has knife out or 1+ bullets loaded

when we can tie:
- we have 2+ bullets reloaded, opp has 2+ bullets reloaded
- we have knife out and 0 bullets, opp has knife out and 0 bullets
